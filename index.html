What are the Leading Causes of Train Service Cancellation and its Financial Impact on Revenue?

Common Keywords in Delay Reasons

Staffing issues led to 238 cancellations, impacting 454 passengers, with 30% requesting refunds amounting to £11,492. Weather, an often unavoidable cause, led to 237 cancellations, impacting 445 passengers, with 30% requesting refunds totaling £11,256. Addressing the primary causes of cancellations, such as signal failures and staffing issues, can significantly reduce the number of cancelled services.
